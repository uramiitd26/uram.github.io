About URAM

The Undergraduate Research and Mentorship (URAM) program at IIT Delhi aims to introduce undergraduate students to research at an early stage of their academic curriculum.

The program provides an opportunity for students to work closely with faculty members on research problems. It helps students develop an understanding of research methodologies and encourages independent thinking.

Objectives

  • To promote research culture among undergraduate students
  • To provide guidance through faculty mentorship
  • To develop analytical and problem-solving skills
  • To encourage interdisciplinary learning

Program Structure

Students selected for the program are assigned to faculty mentors based on their interests and background. They work on defined research problems under supervision.

Regular interaction with mentors is expected. The duration and scope of work depend on the nature of the project.

Eligibility

The program is open to undergraduate students of IIT Delhi. Selection is based on academic performance and interest in research.

Application

Interested students can apply during the announced application period. Details regarding submission and deadlines are notified on the official portal.

Announcements

Applications for the URAM program are invited periodically. Students are advised to check this page for updates and notifications.
