Members

Meet the team behind URAM-IITD.

The Urban and Regional Air Quality Management group brings together faculty, research scholars, and alumni working across air-quality modeling, environmental systems, health studies, and policy-facing research.

  • Faculty Professor-led research in air quality, health, and environmental systems
  • Scholars Doctoral and master's researchers across modeling, climate, and policy
  • Mentoring Postdoctoral, doctoral, master's, and undergraduate mentorship pathways

Faculty

Experienced guidance across environmental research and practice.

The faculty members associated with URAM-IITD bring deep expertise in air-quality modelling, environmental systems, public policy, and engineering practice, helping shape both research direction and real-world impact.

Prof. Mukesh Khare

Faculty Member

Professor Mukesh Khare is a Fellow of the Institution of Engineers India and Fellow of the Wessex Institute of Great Britain. He is a Chartered Engineer and obtained his Ph.D. in the Faculty of Engineering from Newcastle University, UK.

With a specialization in air-quality modelling, his experience spans research and development studies, teaching, consulting, modelling, and editorial activities. He has authored more than 200 research publications and serves on national-level policy and advisory bodies, including the High Level Task Force at the Prime Minister's Office.

Email: mukeshk@civil.iitd.ac.in

Dr. Sri Harsha Kota

Professor | Chair Professor, CERCA

Dr. Sri Harsha Kota is Professor in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at IIT Delhi (since February 2026) and Chair Professor at the Center for Excellence in Research in Clean Air (CERCA), IIT Delhi.

His prior appointments include Associate Professor at IIT Delhi (2021-2026), Assistant Professor at IIT Delhi (2018-2021), Assistant Professor at IIT Guwahati (2014-2018), and Visiting Professor at Louisiana State University, USA.

  • Education: PhD (2014) and MS (2009), Texas A&M University; BE Civil Engineering (2007), Osmania University
  • Research Interests: Urban/regional air quality modeling, PM health burden, source apportionment, indoor air quality, and climate-air quality interactions
  • Publication Metrics: Citations 4432, h-index 33, i10-index 54
Email: harshakota@iitd.ac.in Website: web.iitd.ac.in/~harshakota/

Research Scholar
